The Flash

Based on the TV series.

Welcome to The Flash website.

This page was designed to introduce The Flash which was developed by Greg Berlanti, Andrew Kreisberg and Geoff Johns. You can find more information about the cast. The series premiered in the United States on October 7, 2014, on The CW television network. It is a spin-off from Arrow, existing in the same universe. The first season follows police forensic investigator Barry Allen who is gifted with super speed after being struck by lightning. He is assisted by S.T.A.R. Labs' Dr. Caitlin Snow, Cisco Ramon, and Dr. Harrison Wells in attempting to learn more about his powers and how to use them to be a hero. Barry also attempts to solve the strange murder of his mother by a superhuman attacker when he was young, which put his father in prison, leaving Detective Joe West and his daughter Iris West to take in a young Barry.
